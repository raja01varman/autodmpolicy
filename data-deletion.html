Overview

autodm complies with Meta’s User Data Deletion Policy. This page explains how users can request deletion of data collected through Facebook and Instagram integrations.

1. Automatic Data Deletion

If you remove the autodm app from your Facebook or Instagram account settings, all data associated with your account will be automatically deleted from our systems within 30 days.

2. Manual Data Deletion Request

You may also request manual deletion of your data by sending an email with the subject line “Data Deletion Request” to:

Email: raja.1798varma@gmail.com

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your Instagram or Facebook User ID
  • The account or email associated with your connection
  • Any additional details needed to identify your data

We will delete all related data within 7–30 days and confirm completion by email.

3. Types of Data Deleted

  • Instagram or Facebook User IDs
  • Webhook event logs
  • Automated DM and comment interaction data
  • Technical logs (timestamps, IP addresses)

All deleted data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
